What does Cold-Pressed mean ?

A hydraulic press to extract juice from your favourite fruit and vegetables, creating Cold-Pressed juice.

High pressure processing preserves more of the fruit and veggies that are usually lost during traditional juicing process.
This results in a healthier, fuller glass of juice for your juice cleanse.

The cold pressing process is labor intensive, so you get every bit of the delicious juice.

Cold-Pressed juice preserves more Vitamins, Enzymes, Minerals , and Antioxidants yielding the best possible flavour than regular juice.
